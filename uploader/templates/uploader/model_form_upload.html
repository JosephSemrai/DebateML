Project Description

What is this for?

We hope to decrease the amount of inequality among Debate students by providing a set of ever-adapting resources available to everyone, free of charge.

What am I doing by uploading my speeches?

When you upload your speech, you are adding your speech to a huge dataset of other speeches which will be used to train the neural network on what a Debate speech is, underlying patterns of Debate speeches, how to write one, etc.

What are you looking for?

We are currently looking for Congress speeches.

FAQ

How do I upload speeches on Google Docs?

  1. Open the Google Docs Document
  2. Click "File" on the top bar
  3. Click "Download As"
  4. Click Microsoft Word or OpenDocument Format (ODT)
  5. Drag the downloaded file to the box

Do you store any personal information?

We do not store any information beside the speech that you upload. By uploading this speech, you give us permission to use your speech to train neural networks and use it in any capacity. We will never sell or use your speeches. This project is open source and can be contributed to by anyone on the GitHub repository.
